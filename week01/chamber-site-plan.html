Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Rexburg Chamber of Commerce serves as a community for local businesses to get and grow together. We develop connections and raise awareness for local businesses using a variety of promotions, events, gatherings, and more.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find all the businesses involved in the chamber?
  • Every business and their provided information is listed in the Directory page on the site.
  • Where can I see upcoming events?
  • Upcoming events are shown on the main page.
  • How can I join the Chamber of Commerce?
  • You can join by visiting the Join page on the site.
  • Where can I see the business spotlight?
  • The business spotlight is shown on the main page.
